Are you ready for this year's Apple Harvest Festival ?

This year's Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is hold at September 30 to October 2, 2016. The great local products including apples, fresh baked goods, apple cider donuts and kettle corn will be sold. Great shows and entertainments will be shown to the audience, including games, live performances and juggling.
